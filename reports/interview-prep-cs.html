Interview Prep CS

Mobile-first flashcards PWA with 657 hand-authored cards for senior engineering interviews. Primary target: Mysten Labs (master thesis employer, Sep 2026).

Next.js 16 TypeScript strict Tailwind v4 shadcn/ui Framer Motion Fuse.js Zod PWA Bun + Biome

The Challenge

Senior interview prep needs more than LeetCode grinds.

Staff/senior engineering loops test judgment: distributed systems trade-offs, production horror stories, code review eye, communication. Generic LeetCode sites index coding questions, not the meta-skills. Nothing targets the specific company you want — in this case, Mysten Labs and its Sui stack.

Interview Prep CS is a curated corpus of 657 flashcards spanning algorithms, system design, backend/infra, blockchain, Mysten Labs specifics, ML/AI, OS/net/sec, senior signals, and behavioral. Every card ships with front, back, and optional senior_nuance + quote_to_say for verbal delivery.

Why It Matters

  • Mysten Labs dedicated section: 60 cards covering founders (Evan Cheng, Sam Blackshear, Niyi Abiodun, George Danezis, Kostas Chalkias), Sui architecture, Move, Narwhal/Bullshark/Mysticeti consensus, zkLogin, privacy roadmap. Backed by MYSTEN_RESEARCH.md with inline source citations.
  • Senior signals framed explicitly: 50 dedicated cards on ambiguity handling, scope negotiation, technical disagreement, incident narrative.
  • Mock interview mode: 387 mock-eligible cards feed a timed simulation — not just flip cards, but answer against the clock.
  • SM-2 lite spaced repetition: localStorage-backed review scheduling — return to weak cards automatically.
  • Offline-ready PWA: manual service worker — prep in planes, trains, subway.
