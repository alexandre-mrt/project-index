MA4 Catch-Up

Consolidated study site for FIN-420 (Financial Intermediation) and FIN-424 (Macroeconomics and Monetary Policy), Spring 2026 at EPFL.

Next.js 15 TypeScript Tailwind CSS Biome Bun Vercel

The Challenge

Two dense finance courses, 68 PDFs, and no unified entry point.

FIN-420 and FIN-424 span hundreds of slides, lecture notes, and problem sets. Studying via the EPFL portal means hunting through filenames, losing track of progress, and never seeing how concepts interconnect across the two courses.

MA4 Catch-Up ingests every course artefact, produces typed concept and glossary JSON, and renders them as a navigable, anchor-linked site with formula legends. Progress, theme, and read-state persist in localStorage.

Why It Matters

  • Single catalogue: 68 PDFs indexed under two manifests (fin420-manifest, fin424-manifest), browsable by week and topic.
  • Concept index with TOC: wave-5 feature — anchor navigation across every defined concept, so related ideas in FIN-420 and FIN-424 cross-link cleanly.
  • Glossary + formula legends: every macro and intermediation formula ships with its variable legend, eliminating the "what does Xt stand for again?" friction.
  • Built for exam velocity: this catch-up site targets efficient review during exam prep, not initial learning.

How It Works

Data Pipeline

  • Ingest: PDFs land in public/resources/, manifests in data/.
  • Parse: loader reads manifests + concept/content/glossary JSON at build time.
  • Render: App Router pages pull typed data, render anchor-linked sections.
  • Persist: localStorage for progress + theme.
